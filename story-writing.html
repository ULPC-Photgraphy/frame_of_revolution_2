Story Writing Submission

Theme: Protest graffiti is more than paint on walls—it’s the unheard scream of a movement. Write a micro-story (under 300 words) imagining the untold tale behind real graffiti from the July 2024 protests. Who left this mark? What were they fighting for? What happens when the walls talk back? No experience needed—just rage, hope, and a pen. The best stories will be posted on our official Facebook page, and the top three winners will be awarded on 36 July 2025.
