About the Event

Frame of Revolution: Bangladesh 2.0 is a collaborative visual storytelling initiative that captures the spirit, struggle, and environmental impact of the July 2024 student movement. Through photography, art, and video, this event seeks to spotlight youth resistance and raise awareness about the ecological consequences of state repression.

Main Segments

Mobile Photography

Theme: Anything related to the July protest. Capture raw, unfiltered moments that reflect the intensity, emotion, and unity of the movement—right from your mobile device.

DSLR Photography

Theme: Anything related to the July protest. Use your lens to deliver powerful, artistic interpretations of protest scenes, student solidarity, and the evolving revolution.

Story Writing: Screams Without Voices

Theme: Protest graffiti is more than paint on walls—it’s the unheard scream of a movement. Write a micro-story (under 300 words) imagining the untold tale behind real graffiti from the July 2024 protests. Who left this mark? What were they fighting for? What happens when the walls talk back? No experience needed—just rage, hope, and a pen. The best stories will be posted on our official Facebook page, and the top three winners will be awarded on 36 July 2025.

Special Segments – Environmental Awareness Through Creative Protest

Poster Design Segment

Theme: "Tear Gas in the Air, Resistance on the Ground". Design a striking poster that explores how the use of tear gas, rubber bullets, and other militarized tools against students contributes to environmental degradation. Show how violence affects not just people, but also the air, soil, and ecosystem—revealing the hidden costs of repression.

Video Content Segment

Theme: "Toxic Repression: A Threat to Both Life and Nature". Create a short video—documentary or creative—that illustrates how state-enforced violence (tear gas, sound grenades, baton charges) harms both students and the environment. Let your narrative expose the deeper damage to human rights and ecological balance.
