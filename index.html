About the Event

Frame of Revolution: Bangladesh 2.0 is a collaborative visual storytelling initiative that captures the spirit, struggle, and environmental impact of the July 2024 student movement. Through photography, art, and video, this event seeks to spotlight youth resistance and raise awareness about the ecological consequences of state repression.

Event Dates: 7 July to 35 July 2025
